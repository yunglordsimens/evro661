**Vikusia** is just a girl from Ukraine who came to Prague in 2022 to
study at the Academy of Fine Arts (AVU). She wanted to become a painter,
but people started calling her a violinist, so she became an
interdisciplinary artist working across different media.

Using a variety of acoustic and electronic instruments, she blends
delicate melodies with electronic soundscapes. Her music contrasts
gentle harmonies with expressive poetry filled with reflections on the
modern world --- a world that, in her view, is slowly sliding toward
catharsis.

Her sound moves between contemporary classical musiccc and ambient.
