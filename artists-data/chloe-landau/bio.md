Chloe Landau is a Ukrainian singer from the underground music scene. Her work blends light pop melodies with themes of war, codependent relationships, and death. Her lyrics are intimate and reflective in nature. For Chloe, the creative process serves a therapeutic function and acts as a way to process and reinterpret personal experience. Her performances often take on a performative format, characterized by emotional openness and vulnerability in front of the audience. Over the past year, she has performed at MOFO (Porto), Pandora Gallery (Berlin), the Open Secret event at Otel’ (Kyiv), Club K41, and Abo Records. In February 2026, Chloe released her debut album *Dreamhouse* on the label Standard Deviation.
